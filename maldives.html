Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and luxurious overwater bungalows. It offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, with opportunities for snorkeling, diving, and other water activities. Its famous for the stunning coral reefs, vibrant marine life, and its luxurious resorts that provide an unforgettable experience in the heart of the Indian Ocean.
